The Road Safety Problem

Road Safety around the school has become a growing concern, with learners facing daily risks when crossing nearby roads. Unsafe driver behavior and limited infrastructure contribute significantly to this issue.

Problem Overview

Learners around the school dont follow road rules and cross roads unsafely which leads to acidents happening, drivers themselves dont obey the road rules and put learners at risk, such as an incident some weeks ago as a learner was hit by a car who thankfully was not injured has set the spark towards the concern of road safety around the school.

The lack of street patrols, traffic lights, visible signs and awareness contribute heavily to the issues of road safety.

Root causes

Most drivers dont obey the road rules around the school which leads to them speeding and potentiallly injuring learners that try to cross the road.

Some drivers dont park in the designated parking area and congest the road making it difficult for other drivers to pass through causing some serious issues.

There are cases of learners that around the school entrance and as they do so they move towards the road and leave themselves prone to danger.

Impact on learners

Learners are at risk of injury.

Unsafe road acts disrupts their daily routine.

inwhich the learners develops fear and axiety.

This inturn creates an unsafe school environment for the learners.

Why this needs urgent attention

Without anyway of enforcing road safety around the school drivers will continue to disobey the road rules making the school area an unsafe place.

Evidence

To better understand the scale of this issue, a survey was conducted among learners to gather their experiences and perceptions of road safety.
