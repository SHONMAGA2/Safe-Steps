Problem Snapshot

Road safety has become a huge concern here at hoerskool jan viljoen as learners are at risk at being involed in an accident.

We aim to improve road safety with awareness campaigns, scholar patrols and improved road signs

Research indicates that approximately 40% of car accidents occur due to inadequate, missing, or improperly placed road traffic safety signs See here on a texas report!

Why it matters

Learners everyday are at risk of being involved in an accident due to road laws not being followed or learners themselves not abiding to those road laws or in adiquate road signs.

learner safety is our greates prioty and main goal of our campaign.

And removing the fear of crossing the road is one of our many goals.
