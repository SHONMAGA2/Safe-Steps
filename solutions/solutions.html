Proposed Solutions

Based on survey findings and observed issues, the following solutions aim to improve road safety around the school and reduce the risk faced by learners.

Immediate Actions

  • Raise awareness among learners about safe road crossing practices.
  • Encourage learners to use designated crossing areas.
  • Organise short awareness talks after school.
  • Place temporary road safety posters near school entrances and high-risk crossing areas

School-Based Solutions

  • Introduce a scholar patrol system.
  • Assign teachers or staff to monitor peak traffic times.
  • Educate learners through assemblies or class discussions.

Infrastructure Improvements

  • Install clear and visible road safety signs near the school.
  • Request pedestrian crossings or traffic lights.
  • Improve road markings for better visibility.

Driver & Community Awareness

  • Engage with parents and local drivers about safe driving near schools.
  • Distribute awareness messages through school channels.
  • Encourage responsible parking and reduced speed near the school.

Safe Steps Campaign Actions

  • Create and share educational videos on road safety.
  • Document unsafe areas and present findings to school leadership.
  • Organise awareness activities and demonstrations.
  • Use survey data to support requests for improvements.

Expected Impact

These solutions aim to reduce risky behaviour, improve awareness, and create a safer environment for learners. By combining education, enforcement, and infrastructure improvements, the overall safety of the school area can be significantly improved.
